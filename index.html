— About —

Hi.

My name is Mark Centoni, and I’m a Front End Developer with a B2B content marketing history.

After spending 10 years in B2B content marketing, content strategy and content creation, I developed a curious obsession with coding. As a marketer, I worked on a wide range of projects: from blogging to marcom design, social media marketing, email marketing and template development, landing page development, marketing automation implementation, website management, campaign management and analytics.

The coding bug bit me while slinging HTML and CSS in blogging and email tools, so I jumped at every chance I could find to code in my day job while picking up some freelance web design work on the side. But I wanted to really learn how code like a pro. Like, you know, program.

So I trained with the great folks at General Assembly SF in Front End (HTML5/CSS3/Javascript) and Back End Web Development (Ruby/Rails). I work in:

  • HTML5/CSS3/Javascript
  • Sass, jQuery
  • Illustrator/Photoshop/Indesign
  • PHP/MySQL/Wordpress
  • With experience in Ruby/Rails

I enjoy working on hand lettering projects and cooking in my spare time. There’s something about the mix of organic lines and measured structure in hand lettering that I find soothing. Cooking holds a similar yin/yang pleasure, but with less measurement and more wine.

*P.S. this site uses a custom WordPress theme built with Bootstrap, Sass, Shuffle.js and love.
